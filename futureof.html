About

I am a Chinese/Trini who was born and raised in New York. I graduated from Fordham University with a degree in both Entrepreneurship and Management. As long as I can remember I have always been building. I like to believe my passion began in elementary school when I started spending my summers building houses in Trinidad with my dad. My love for technology developed during college when I interned, and later worked full time, at plyfe.

At plyfe I met irreplaceable mentors who would later invite me join their family at "Adam & Luna". Since then I have had the pleasure of helping those that mean the most to us by building products that can best reflect the passion of their work. My favorite thing about what I do is creating a unique and unforgettable experience for our users.
