Resume








Hi! My name is Christopher Chin Fong and I am looking to join the IDEO team as the Business Designer – Product. I have always wanted to be part of the IDEO team, ever since watching a video of your company in my Entrepreneurship class at Fordham University. My past experience has been heavily focused on Product Management and building products with a great user experience. I have been blessed to have been able to help build two companies in the past couple of years. One was an iPhone App and the other was a web/mobile consulting agency.

I have strong knowledge in Product Management and Organizational Behavior. Other skills I possess are design (Photoshop, Illustrator, wireframing), front end engineering (Html, Css), operations, product development, Start-ups, marketing, finance and analytics. I got into product management, because I love being able to work on something from ideation to the end. At my last company StoryApp, I was able to do this with an incredible team of Artiest and Engineers that have become my second family. We enjoyed every moment building the product making sure everything was intuitive and user friendly.

I learned a lot building StoryApp. It was the first company I was a part of since inception, so I had to teach myself a lot of new skills. The first and one of the most important things I learned while growing our family was we had to look for people who were not only passionate about our product, but could get along with every member of our family. I also taught myself how to code and design so that I can better communicate with our artist and engineers in our family. Another thing I learned during this time was when building a product, you need to go out and talk to your potential users. You need to understand the way they think and get in the mindset of how they would use your product. I spent many days at meet up groups, on Skype conversations, and in coffee shops just getting to know our users. I have enjoyed every moment building StoryApp and I want to use everything I have learned to contribute to the IDEO team.

Below are just some of the projects I have been lucky to be a part of recently. Thank you for taking the time to visit my website I built for IDEO.
